Zunshayn Apparels is an agent / buying house specialized in garment, textiles and accessories with offices in Philippine, Dhaka (Bangladesh). Zunshayn Apparels was established to create more transparency and reliability and with the confidence that, once reliability can be provided regarding the required price and quality standards, there are ample business opportunities in Asia. We are confident of accomplishing our vision and can work with a wide range of companies through our diverse array of services.

We are able to provide and preserve our high service levels owing to our highly educated and experienced European management, good local organizational structure, and our detailed knowledge of the local market and its possibilities. Our service encompasses two major areas; the first is where we operate as an agent for our clients, developing a tailor-made service and guiding the client through the entire production process from sourcing and ordering up to the correct shipment of the goods. The second area is where we act as a trader, and as such we operate as a direct manufacturer of all sorts of garments and accessories.

As trade globalization gains pace, sourcing from distant countries and new suppliers is a growing trend. Under these conditions, monitoring the production process requires an on-the-spot presence to reduce the risk of substandard quality, discrepancies, shipment delays, and other deviations from purchase order requirements.

Given our extensive knowledge of the European and Asian markets, our services provide our clients with significant and substantial added value.

We look forward to serving you.
